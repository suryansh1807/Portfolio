Book Review

Harry Potter

Book Name: Harry Potter And the Philosopher's Stone
Author of the book: J.K. Rowling
Publisher: Bloomsbury
Publication Date: 26 June 1997
Pages: 332
Available in: Audio, Hardcover and a movie

Summary

Harry potter's Parent were killed by Dark wizard on the day of his birth and he was adopted by his last relatives Dursley. After 11 years from his birth he started getting letters from nowhere and on his 11 th birthday, A giant came from nowhere and tells him that he is a wizard and he has to study at HOGWARTS SCHOOL OF WITCHCRAFT AND WIZARDRY. He was very supriesed and excited. He finds that he is very famous in wizarding world. He made friends for the first time in his life. On his mysterious adventure he finds that Lord Voldemort is trying to resurrect and he has to stop him.

My Thougth About The Book
The book was my first adventure novel ever. The author of this book did a good job in writting the characters and complicated expressions. I liked the idea of the wizarding world, it was like a real world that coexist with our own world. It was like watching a movie, each and every scene's detail was greatly explained like expressions on the face, buildings, places etc. I recommend this book to young reader as the language is easy to understand.
